Mission


The Students’ Union at Coimbatore Institute of Technology aims to enhance student life in all relevant aspects of academic, social and extra-curricular areas, mediates the gap between the students and the management, encourages and supports active participation of the students to unveil their inborn talents and facilitates all services for the betterment of students.

Vision


The Students’ Union at Coimbatore Institute of Technology seeks to mastermind various programs, services and facilities to uplift formal and informal learning of students and their leadership growth. We will be progressively energetic and innovative with our thoughts and ideas and strive to be an efficacious body in improving the infrastructure of the Institution.
